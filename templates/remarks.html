Welcome to the Valora Music Project!

Before you begin, we'd like to share some important information about this questionnaire and how your data will be used.

This questionnaire will take about 3-4 minutes to complete. It is divided into two sections:

  • Section 1 (PANAS): A 20-item list where you'll rate different feelings.
  • Section 2 (SAM): Two image-based questions to help identify your mood.

Your questionnaire responses will be stored anonymously and used only for this project.

To log in and provide recommendations, this app will use your Spotify account for two purposes only:

  • To authenticate your identity (log you in).
  • To read your existing playlists to help find new music.

Your anonymous mood data will not be publicly linked to your Spotify profile.
